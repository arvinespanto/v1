Assistive Diagnosis Application for Melanoma Screening

Technologies Used:
Screenshots

Project Description:
Objectives:

              The main objective of this project is to create an expert system forming an alternative method for diagnosing melanoma disease, in accordance with engineering standards and consideration of trade-offs based on multiple constraints such as time, performance, and functionality.

The project:

This is an android application that utilizes the camera of their smartphone. Users can use their smartphone to capture different image of their moles or skin patches and my system will process and analyze the captured image then classify it to melanoma or normal skin. An alert will be provided to the user to seek medical help if the mole belongs to atypical or melanoma category.
